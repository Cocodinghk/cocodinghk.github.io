Bio

  • I am currently a second-year PhD student in The Hong Kong Polytechnic University. Before that, I received BEng in Computer Science in Tianjin University in 2024. Prior to PhD study, I was also a Research Assistant at Department of Computing, The Hong Kong Polytechnic University from 2023 to 2024, fortunately supervised by Prof. Bo Li in AlgoPoly Group.


  • My research interest covers algorithmic game theory, which lies in the intersection of Computer Science and Economics. My ongoing research topic is fair allocation and contract design. I believe elegance in mathematics has the power to interpret the world.
